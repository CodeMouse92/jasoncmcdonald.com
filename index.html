About

Jason C McDonald does many things. Writing books is one of them (you can see a few of them just above) and existing outside of time and space, lending his unique abilities to the forces of good found scattered across and between the folds in the fabric of reality is another. He might not realize he does this, but he does it nonetheless. Much of his energy is taken up in this pursuit, so if he seems tired or distracted at times please grant him some grace; he's probably rescuing a baby silulink. Despite all of this, Jason also manages to contribute to many other good causes, like some of the podcasts listed below and various github repositories. Should you meet him or his typewriter on the street, say hi, stay a while, and listen.
